Details

This repository exists for three main reasons:

  • To function as a sort of track for my progress in learning mathematics upto an undergraduate level
  • To contain my notes and exercises as I work through various books
  • For the reference of other people who may want to teach themselves mathematics

My goal is to work through an undergraduate level course worth of Mathematics, before finishing my A-levels (1 year). I highly doubt I will succeed, but when setting goals it's nice to be optimistic, no? Works as a motivator.

Because I will be doing this in tandem with my A-levels, and the main focus in the A-levels Mathematics syllabus around this time is Calculus, I will not be studying Calculus on my own outside of school. Meaning, it will not be part of this track.

NOTE: This plan is highly theoretical, at least for now. It's a compilation of internet wisdom, and not really a complete work yet. It is continuously being updated.
